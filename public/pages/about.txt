# About

Hi, I'm Lihan Badenhorst.

I build software, AI systems, robotics projects, and electronics. Most of my time is spent designing new ideas, writing code, or experimenting with hardware to see what I can make next. I enjoy solving difficult problems and learning new technologies that help me build better systems.
